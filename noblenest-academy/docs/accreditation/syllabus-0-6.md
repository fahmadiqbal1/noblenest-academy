# Early-Years Curriculum Map — Ages 0–6

Noble Nest Academy's early-years programme follows an integrated domain framework drawing on EYFS (UK), the Reggio Emilia approach, and Montessori prepared-environment principles.

## Developmental Domains

| Domain | Focus areas |
|---|---|
| Physical & Motor | Gross motor milestones, fine-motor tracing & drawing, sensory exploration |
| Language & Literacy | Phonics readiness, listening comprehension, multilingual exposure (EN / AR / ZH / JA) |
| Cognitive | Number sense, shape & space, cause-and-effect reasoning, memory games |
| Social-Emotional | Self-regulation, empathy through storytelling, turn-taking, emotions vocabulary |
| Creative Arts | Music & movement, drawing canvas, guided craft activities |
| Cultural & Values | Islamic ethics, Japanese Ikigai-for-kids, Scandinavian *lagom*, Chinese filial piety |

## Age-Band Progression

### 0–12 months (Newborn / Infant)
- **Primary vehicle:** Maternal module (parent-led). Content in `maternal/` routes.
- Sensory songs, black-and-white visual stimulation, tummy-time guidance.
- Assessment: parent-reported milestone check-ins (no screen required).

### 12–24 months (Toddler)
- Short-form video lessons (≤ 3 min), song-and-movement activities.
- Vocabulary building: 20-word target sets per 3-month block (EN + one heritage language).
- Motor: tracing-canvas finger activities, stacking/sorting drag-and-match.

### 2–3 years (Toddler extended)
- Guided-steps activities with parent narrator prompt.
- Introduction to numbers 1–10 via song, manipulative analogue.
- Emotional literacy: "How does Noor feel?" story-based quizzes.

### 3–4 years (Preschool)
- Quiz activities (4-option, image-heavy).
- Drawing canvas with shape templates.
- Cultural module onboarding (first structured cultural lesson).

### 4–5 years (Pre-K)
- Assessment battery (discovery battery): IQ-adjacent spatial tasks, verbal analogies.
- STEM readiness: pattern blocks, simple code-blocks drag-and-match.
- Phonics: letter tracing + sound-match activity.

### 5–6 years (Kindergarten)
- Full activity-player suite: quiz, assessment, code-blocks, drawing, tracing.
- Reading: guided decodable text with word-click audio.
- First Blockly activity (sequencing loops).

## Curriculum Coverage Notes
- Each age band targets ≥ 10 activities before a new band is unlocked.
- Activities link to `ActivityCoverageTest` gating assertions (`tests/Feature/ActivityCoverageTest.php`).
