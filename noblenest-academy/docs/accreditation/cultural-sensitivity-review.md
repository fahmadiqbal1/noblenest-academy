# Cultural Sensitivity Review — Noble Nest Academy

External advisor sign-off on Japanese / Chinese / Scandinavian / Islamic modules.

## Review Process

All cultural content follows a three-gate review:

1. **Subject-matter expert (SME) draft** — curriculum team member with heritage connection to the culture writes the initial activity content.
2. **Community advisor review** — an advisor from the relevant cultural community reviews for accuracy, tone, and potential offence.
3. **Editorial sign-off** — curriculum lead approves the final text and images before seeding.

## Cross-Cultural Principles (All Modules)

1. No culture is presented as superior to any other.
2. Religious content is always opt-in via parent settings (coming Q3 2026).
3. Images reviewed for appropriate representation (no stereotyping, diverse skin tones).
4. All content reviewed against the UN Convention on the Rights of the Child, Article 29 (education that respects cultural identity).
5. Community representatives receive a content-credit acknowledgement on the platform's About page.
